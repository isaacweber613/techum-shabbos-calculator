Reading the audit view
  1. Solid shape: the exact mapped building footprint.
  2. Dotted shape: 70⅔ amos from its walls. Touching a house joins the chain.
  3. Dashed box: a derived settlement after chain and city-merge rules.
  4. Bright box: your selected city’s ribua; the techum is measured from it.
